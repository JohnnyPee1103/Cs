Elige
Jonathan "EliGE" Jablonowski started his Counter-Strike: Global Offensive career in the end of 2014 with SapphireKelownaDotCom. But with SapphireKelownaDotCom he didn't win any tournament. His team failed to qualify for MLG X Games Aspen Invitational. In the beginning of 2015 Jonathan joined eLevate. His first achievement happened at Clutch Con 2015 where eLevate took 3rd-4th place, losing to Fnatic. After an outstanding performance at iBUYPOWER Invitational Spring 2015, Jonathan joined Team Liquid. His first tournament in Team Liquid was Alienware Area 51 Cup #1 where team took second place losing to his former team eLevate. In April Jonathan took 1 place at RGN Intercontinental Open 2 North America and in May at ESWC 2015 - MSI USA Qualifier. In September American team won CS:GO Championship Series: Season 1 - Group Stage, iBUYPOWER Invitational 2015 - Fall and qualified at DreamHack Open Cluj-Napoca 2015.
